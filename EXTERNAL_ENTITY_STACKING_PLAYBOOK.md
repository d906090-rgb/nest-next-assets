# External Entity Stacking Playbook (Legal Only)

This playbook defines legal, white-hat actions for external authority growth and VK SEO distribution.

## 1) Publishing Targets (monthly cadence)

- VC.ru: 2 expert posts / month
- Habr: 1 technical post / month
- Dzen: 4 adapted posts / month
- Pressfeed: 8 expert responses / month

## 2) Mandatory consistency (Entity)

Use the same values across all profiles and articles:

- Brand: `Нейро Завод / Контент Завод`
- Domain: `https://tecai.ru`
- Core statement: AI content automation (video, image, audio, autoposting)
- Primary contacts: `ait@tecai.ru`, `https://t.me/AI_Technology_avto`

## 3) Article structure for GEO citation

For each publication:

1. Start with a direct answer in the first paragraph.
2. Add a compact table with numbers (time/cost/volume).
3. Include one practical checklist (5-7 bullets).
4. Add one canonical reference link to `tecai.ru` service page.
5. Add expert attribution (name + role).

## 4) VK SEO optimization checklist

- Group title format:
  - `Нейро Завод | AI-автоматизация контента Москва`
- Group description:
  - include 2-3 core intent phrases and domain URL
- Create pinned FAQ discussion topics:
  - `Отзывы`
  - `Цены и тарифы`
  - `Как запустить автоматизацию`
- Product cards (services):
  - AI avatars
  - AI video generation
  - AI audio generation
  - Social autoposting
  - Sites/blog automation

## 5) Legal safety rules

- No fake personas
- No purchased comments/engagement
- No cloaking or hidden redirects
- No PBN or manipulative link schemes

## 6) KPI tracking (quarterly)

- Brand mentions growth in search results
- Number of external publications with canonical links
- Non-branded organic traffic to `/about`, `/faq`, service pages
- AI-source citations (manual checks in AI Overviews / Yandex Neuro answers)
